# Content Data

Blog posts and pages are generated by `scripts/06-import-content.sh`.

## Blog Posts

The script creates posts about:
- Hiking tips and guides
- Gear reviews
- Trail recommendations
- Camping advice
- Safety information

## Pages

Standard e-commerce pages:
- About Us
- Contact
- Shipping & Returns
- FAQ
- Terms and Conditions

## Customization

Edit `scripts/06-import-content.sh` to add custom content.
